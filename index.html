Who We Are

Greenlinx is an Egypt-based export business specializing in premium fruits and vegetables — both fresh and frozen. We serve clients across Europe, the Gulf, and Africa with quality-assured produce, strong cold chain logistics, and a commitment to consistency.

Our sourcing network includes trusted farms in the Nile Delta and Upper Egypt, and we deliver tailored solutions for every client — from bulk shipping to custom-packaged exports.
