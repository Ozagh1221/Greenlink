Product Overview

We supply premium Egyptian mango varieties selected for sweetness, texture, and sturdiness in transit. Fresh options are harvested at optimal maturity; IQF cubes and slices are processed rapidly to lock in flavor, color, and nutrients—ideal for retail, foodservice, and industrial applications.

Specifications

  • Origin: Egypt (Nile Delta & key growing regions)
  • Processing: Fresh hand-picked / IQF cubes 10–20 mm, slices
  • Calibers / Cuts: Fresh 5–15 cm; IQF 10×10–20×20 mm; custom cuts on request
  • QC: Metal detection, foreign-matter control, temperature checks
  • Shelf Life: Fresh 10–14 days (2–4 °C); IQF 24 months (≤ −18 °C)
  • Storage: Fresh 2–4 °C; IQF −18 °C
