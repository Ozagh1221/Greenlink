About Greenlinx

Greenlinx exists to elevate Egypt’s agricultural potential and connect it to the world. We work hand-in-hand with trusted farmers and producers to export the highest quality fruits and vegetables across the Gulf, Europe, and Africa. With a sharp focus on freshness, logistics, and long-term partnerships, we are building the next-generation export platform. Our goal is to lead the export sector in Egypt—not just in agriculture, but across the full spectrum of food products. Greenlinx is not just a middleman—we are a growth partner for producers and a reliability hub for buyers.
